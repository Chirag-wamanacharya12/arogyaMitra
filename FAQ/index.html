Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

General Questions

1. What is [Health Portal Name]?

[Health Portal Name] is an all-in-one platform for managing your health. You can book appointments, order prescriptions, track wellness goals, access research resources, and more.

2. How do I create an account on the health portal?

To create an account, click the 'Sign Up' button on the homepage, enter your email and password, and follow the prompts. Depending on your role (Patient, Physician, etc.), additional information may be required.

3. What types of users are supported on the platform?

We support six types of users: Patients, Physicians, Practitioners, Researchers, Service Providers, and Health Content Creators.

Patient Services

4. What services are available for patients?

Patients can book doctor appointments, order medications, track health progress, and access personalized wellness tips.

5. How do I book an appointment with a doctor?

After logging in, go to the 'Appointments' section, select your preferred doctor, choose an available time slot, and confirm the booking.

6. Can I get prescriptions through the health portal?

Yes, once your doctor prescribes a medication, you can order it through our integrated pharmacy service and have it delivered to your home.

Subscriptions & Plans

7. What subscription plans are available?

We offer four subscription types: Free, Basic, Premium, and Exclusive, each with different levels of access to services and features.
